Welcome to ML-VXM!

An elegant virtual crossmatch tool. Based on machine learning.

Instructions:

  • Perform the complete HLA typing of the donor at high resolution. If necessary, use a converter.
  • Find the patient's SAB panels having the highest MFI values for the donor's HLA alleles.
  • Fill this form with the highest MFI values for the donor's HLA alleles per locus.
  • If a donor's HLA allele is present in more than one bead, choose its lowest MFI value.
  • If the donor's HLA alleles of a specific locus are not tested, leave its field blank.
